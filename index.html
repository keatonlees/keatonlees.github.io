Hi! I'm Keaton

I'm a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo.

I'm an enthusiastic learner who loves to work on numerous different projects. Systems Design Engineering has opened my eyes to the possiblity of different engineering fields, each contributing to the future of engineering in their own way. I am someone who loves to both work with software and hardware as I believe that both are equally important in any situation; I enjoy combining both aspects together in order to achieve an end goal.

Outside of school, you can find me doing 3D-design and 3D-printing my own objects, doing an Arduino electronics project, going to the gym and running, or playing the piano and drums. I am also quite fond of travelling and exploring the world.
