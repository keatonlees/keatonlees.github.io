Hi! I'm Keaton

I'm a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo.

I'm an enthusiastic learner who loves to work on numerous projects. Systems Design Engineering has opened my eyes to the possibility of different engineering fields, each contributing to the future of engineering in their own way. I am someone who loves to both work with software and hardware as I believe that both are equally important; I enjoy combining both aspects in order to achieve a final end goal.

Outside of school, you can find me working on 3D-design and 3D-printing my own creations, Arduino electronics projects, going to the gym, track and field, volleyball, or playing the piano and drums. I am also fond of travelling and exploring the world; I have been to Taiwan, Australia, Southeast Asia, France, and UK.
